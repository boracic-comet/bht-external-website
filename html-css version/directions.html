Directions

From the South using Interstate 65

  1. Follow I-65 to Louisville.
  2. Take exit 23 from I-265.
  3. Continue on Taylorsville Road.
  4. Drive to S. English Station Road.

From the South using Interstate 75

  1. Take I-75N to US-25W N in Caryville. Take exit 134 from I-75N.
  2. Get on I-75 N in Jellico.
  3. Continue on I-75 N to Shelby County.
  4. Take exit 28 from I-64W.
  5. Follow State Hwy 1399 and KY-148 W to S. English Station Road.

From the East

  1. Follow I-64 W to Louisville.
  2. Take exit 28 from I-64 W.
  3. Follow State Highway 1399 and KY-148 W to S. English Station Road.

From the West

  1. Follow I-64 E to Louisville.
  2. Take exit 23 from I-265 W / KY-841 W/Gene Snyder Freeway.
  3. Continue on Taylorsville Road. Drive to S. English Station Road.
