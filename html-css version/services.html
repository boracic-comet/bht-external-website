Environmental and Air Filtration Services

At Blue Heaven Technologies we strive to provide quality air filtration and environmental testing.

Services Offered:

  • Air Filtration Testing
  • Environmental/Emissions Testing
  • Test Dusts

Clean Room

We have a clean room for HEPA/ULPA filter testing in factory frames. The clean room can be used to test Clean Air Delivery Rates (CADR) for room air cleaners and other indoor air cleaning devices. We can test vacuum cleaner efficiency in a clean room environment as well.

Security

Don't want your stored produce exposed where your competition can see it? We now have a concealed and locked storage area for both products waiting to be tested (in a conditioned space) and for products that have been tested prior (covered and protected from the elements).
